FAQ

What criteria should I prioritize when selecting a productivity tool?
Focus on core functionalities, user-friendliness, and fit for your team size.

How can I ensure my team adopts new productivity tools effectively?
Implement thorough training and integrate regular feedback sessions.

How long should I trial a productivity tool?
A minimum of 14 days is recommended for a complete assessment.

What’s a common mistake when evaluating productivity tools?
Choosing based solely on popularity rather than actual team needs.

How often should I reassess the productivity tools we use?
Aim for a monthly review cycle, especially during the initial implementation phase.
