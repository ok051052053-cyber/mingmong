FAQ

What is the easiest passive income stream to start?
Creating an online course or blog can be among the simplest paths, allowing you to leverage existing expertise.

How much time do I need to invest initially to see results?
Expect to dedicate about 5-10 hours a week for the first month during setup, and perhaps 1-3 hours weekly for ongoing maintenance.

What common mistakes should I avoid?
Underestimating effort and ignoring hidden costs are prevalent pitfalls that many beginners face.

Are there passive income strategies that require little money?
Affiliate marketing often requires minimal upfront costs, mostly just a website and marketing efforts.
